Computer engineering is a branch of electrical engineering and computer science that integrates several fields of computer science and electronic engineering required to develop computer hardware and software.

  • It engages with software development.

Computer engineering is a program designed to meet the rapidly expanding demand for engineers with strong design skills. It is detail-oriented, analytical, excellent problems solvers, and able to identify, analyze and craft solutions to complex problems.
